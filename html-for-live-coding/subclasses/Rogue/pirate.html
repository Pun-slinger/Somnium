Rogue

Pirate

Born among the Boundless Ocean, your youth is spent under the wings of the scalawags of the ocean. They taught you the lifestyle of a ruthless survivor among these waters filled with savages. Now, like the water you sail upon, you are free from obstacles that blocks your path and with the wind behind your back, the sky is the limit.

On The Move

Starting at 3rd level, you can drink potions using a bonus action. In addition, you gain a climbing and swimming speed equal to your walking speed.

Savage Strike

Also starting from when you choose this subclass at 3rd level, you don't need the Finesse property on a weapon to use your Sneak Attack if that weapon is being wielded in one hand. All other restrictions for that class feature still apply.

Trash Talker

At 9th level, you can use your bonus action to attempt to challenge an enemy. The target must be within 30 ft of you that you can see and must make a Wisdom (Insight) check contested by your Charisma (Persuasion) check. If your check succeeds, you have advantage on your next attack against that target and it had disadvantage on its next attack against you. If your check fails, the creature gains the bonus.

Slippery Tricks

At 13th level, when you use your Uncanny Dodge to reduce the damage from a melee attack from a creature that is within 5 ft of you, you can immediately make a melee attack against that creature.

Feared Legend

At 17th level, you emit a 15 ft radius aura of fear. Enemies entering the aura for the first time must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC = 8 + your Proficiency bonus + your Charisma modifier) or be Frightened of you and can not move until the end of your next turn. In addition, you have advantage on attacks against creatures frightened of you by the aura.
