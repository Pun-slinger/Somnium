Cleric

Emotion Domain

The Emotion Domain focuses on the core of what makes every living creature unique: the various emotions they experienced. Collectively known as the Prismatic Pantheon, the Emotional Beings each govern one of the seven primary emotions of the emotional spectrum. While each have different views on matters and promote a variety of values to be treasured by their worshipers, clerics of the Emotion Domain are conduits that channels the very essence of their Being and guide others that following one's heart is not foolish but freeing.

Domain Spells

At each indicated cleric level, add the listed spells to your spells prepared. They do not count towards your limit.

Cleric Level Spells
1st Charm Person, Hellish Rebuke
3rd Calm Emotions, Suggestion
5th Beacon of Hope, Fear
7th Confusion, Aura of Purity
9th Dominate Person, Hallow

Bonus Proficiency

You gain proficiency in Insight.

Heed My Words

Twice per long rest, you can cast the Command spell without expending a spell slot and as a bonus action.

Channel Divinity: Command Behavior

At 2nd level, as an action, choose a creature that you can see. That creature must make a Wisdom saving throw. If fail, you may make the target feel a certain emotion towards a certain creature. The target becomes charmed by, frightened of or hostiles towards the creature of your choice that you can see. This effect lasts for 1 minute.

Blessing of the Prism

At 6th level, you gain one of the following abilities depending on which Emotional Being from the Prismatic Pantheon you follow

Color Benefit
Red Whenever you cast an attack spell or make a melee attack, you can add your Wisdom modifier to the damage roll.
Orange You gain a 15 ft radius aura. Once per long rest, the first ally to go unconscious while within your aura is instead taken down to 1 HP. In addition, all allies within the aura have advantage on death saving throws.
Yellow As an action, select 1 enemy you can see, that enemy must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, that creature is discouraged against attacking and have disadvantage on attack rolls for 1 minute. At the end of each of the creature’s turn, it can remake the saving throw.
Green Allies within 30 ft of you have advantage on saving throws against being frightened and enemies within this aura has disadvantage against frightened effects.
Blue You may choose 1 additional target when you cast beneficial spells that target multiple allies and all targets of the spell gains temporary hit points equal to your Wisdom modifier.
Pink When an ally adjacent to you is attacked, you can use your reaction to change the attack target to yourself and add your proficiency bonus to your AC.
Purple You can select an enemy within 60 ft that you can see and take away 1 beneficial effect that they have and apply it to yourself (if applicable). You can use this ability once per long rest. In addition, you do 1d4 extra Psychic damage for each beneficial effect active on yourself.

Spectrum's Chosen

Beginning at 8th level, all damage spells you cast now do an additional 1d8 + your Wisdom modifier Psychic damage. In addition, you are immune to all emotion-based effects that would affect you negatively.

Prismatic Herald

At 17th level, once per long rest, you can choose to drop to 1 hit point instead when you would be reduced to 0 hit points. In addition, if you or an ally within 30 ft of you roll a natural 20 on a saving throw, you regain a use of your Channel Divinity.
