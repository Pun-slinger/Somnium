Paladin

Oath of Sacrifice

To swear an Oath of The Void is swearing a commitment to serve the Undeath Queen. Bathe in the darkness of the Void, paladins who swear this oath will be shunned, chastised and feared by many. However, to be swayed by the ideals of those who can not see behind the veil is to be fragile, not what a messenger of the Queen is supposed to represent. You take this oath because you love and care for the world and its people and you are not afraid to fight evil corruption with your own darkness.

Tenets of The Void

The tenets of the Void are held by paladins to safeguard the Void and prevent the balance between it and the material plane from collapsing.

  • Some Secrets are Better Left Unknown: Knowledge is power and it can do unimaginable damage. Keep safe the secrets of the Queen’s domain.

  • Loyalty to the Queen: Do not be sway by gifts or favors. Stay true to your order as a servant of the Undeath Queen.

  • No Mercy for the Uninvited: You are what stands between invaders wanting to cross between The Void and other planes. Snuff them out, with extreme prejudice.

  • Shine in the Dark: Cutia is a kind soul and you should always greet with a smile for nothing is stronger in a time of despair.

Oath Spells

You gain oath spells at the paladin levels listed.

Channel Divinity: Void Field

As an action, you summon a 30ft radius cloud of Void darkness and heavily obscured the area that lasts for 10 minutes. You and any creatures you choose within 5ft of you can see normally in this darkness. Creatures within this zone will have to make attack rolls and Perception checks at disadvantage, even with Darkvision. While in this zone, your weapon deals an extra 1d4 necrotic damage and you are healed for the amount of necrotic damage dealt.

Channel Divinity: The Queen's Command

As a bonus action, select 1 Undead creature within 30ft of you that you can see. That creature must make a Wisdom saving throw or be charmed for 1 minute. This charm effect bypass the charm immunity if that creature has it. The creature can make the saving throw again at the end of each of its turn.

Aura of the Living

At 7th level, you and allies within 10ft of you are resistant to necrotic damage and attacks from Undead creatures within the aura has disadvantage

At 18th level, the range of this aura increase to 30ft

Relentless Darkness

At 15th level, Void Field now follows you and you gain a +1 necrotic damage to your weapon damage roll whenever you defeats an enemy within Void Field.

Void Champion

At 20th level, you become one of the direct servants of Cutia. As an action, you can undergo a transformation. For 1 minute, you gain the following benefits:

  • You are resistant to bludgeoning, slashing and piercing damage and immune to necrotic damage.

  • When you are hit with a melee attack, the attacking creature takes necrotic damage equal to half the damage you taken from the attack.

When you hit a creature, that creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be Frightened of you and have its movement speed reduced to 0 until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, the creature just have its movement speed reduced by half. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest.
