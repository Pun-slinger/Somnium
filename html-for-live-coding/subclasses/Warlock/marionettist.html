Warlock

Marionettist

Your patron is a powerful entity with the power to easily bend the mind, bodies and soul of others to fulfill their ambitions, the Marionettist. Whether you enter this pact willingly or not, all that matters now is that you are no longer in control and when the strings are pulled, you better play your part.

Expanded Spell List

Cleric Level Spells
1st Command, Dissonant Whispers
2nd Rope Trick, Detect Thoughts
3rd Animate Dead, Tiny Servant
4th Compulsion, Dominate Beast
5th Animate Objects, Dominate Person

Ventriloquist

Starting at 1st level, when you cast a spell that requires a verbal component, it is ignored.

Strings Attached

At 1st level, as an action you can select a creature within 60 ft of you that you can see. It must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or fall under your influence. For 5 minutes, you have advantage on all Charisma checks against it.

As an action, you can choose to further extend your grasp on the target and force the target to make another Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, you can force it to move up to half its movement speed to an unoccupied space. If it succeeds, your control is disconnected. Either way, the target knows that you are controlling it and can make an opposed Intelligence check against your own and if it succeeds, the effect ends.

This feature requires concentration to maintain control over the target and you can use this feature once per short or long rest.

Soulless Puppet

Starting at 1st level, when your hit points would drop to 0, you patron can choose to intercept. If unwilling, you must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw to resist the take over. On a failed save or you do not resist you patron’s interference, spectral strings appears and connect to your unconscious body.

When your patron has control of your body, they can drag it up to half your movement in a random direction and make a melee attack roll against the closest target within range. You remain in this state until you regain consciousness and must still make death saves as normal.

Manufacture Marionette

At 6th level, you can spend an action to puppeteer the corpse of a Beast or Humanoid of size medium or smaller that has died for at most 30 days. The body rises up with hit points equal to your Warlock level and uses the Zombie stat block from the Monster Manual. In addition, you gain access to its memories from its last 7 days of life.

In combat, the puppet’s turn goes immediately after yours and it can move up to 60 ft away from you and gain a bonus to its attack roll equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of +0).

The puppet remains in your control until either you are incapacitated, it is reduced to 0 hit points or 1 hour has passed since you took control of it. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of 1).

The More The Merrier

Starting at 10th level, you can now control 2 puppets with Manufacture Marionette. Taking control requires an action for each, but both may be commanded on each of the puppeteer’s turns.

In addition, you now can see through either of the puppet’s eyes and hear what they hear as well. As an action, you can choose to speak through a puppet in any language that you know or the puppet knew when it was alive.

Encroaching Infiltration

At 10th level, when a puppet lands an attack on a creature, you can spend your reaction to spread your influence into the target. It must make a Wisdom saving throw and on a failed save, you can immediately make a knowledge check on the target with advantage.

Master Puppeteer

At 14th level, you can now use Manufacture Marionette on any type of creature and your puppets now uses their own stat blocks instead of the Zombie stat block.

In addition, Manufacture Marionette now can be used on creatures of size Large or smaller. 2 uses of Manufacture Marionette must be expended to raise a Large creature and you can not summon another puppet while the Large size puppet is active.
