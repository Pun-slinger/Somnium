Radiance Pantheon

Alan

Overview

Another of the few deities that is physically manifested on Isox. At first glance, one could easily mistaken him for a common traveler. Described by those who have talk to him as a surprisingly down to earth deity, Alan travel place to place to share feats achieved through friendship that he has witnessed. In Alan's teachings, nothing is more powerful than a bond forged through friendship and absolute trust. So ask yourself, do you trust those you walk along with?
